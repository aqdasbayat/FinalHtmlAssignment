3.Deviled eggs

  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard (or Dijon if you want to get fancy)
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar or lemon juice
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Step by step cooking instructions:

  1. Place 6 eggs in a pot and cover them with cold water (about 1 inch above the eggs).

  3. Once it hits a full boil, turn off the heat, cover the pot, and let it sit for 10‚Äì12 minutes.
  4. Transfer eggs to a bowl of ice water (or run under cold water) for 5‚Äì10 minutes. This stops the cooking and makes peeling easier.

  8. Place the empty egg whites on a serving tray.
  9. In the bowl with yolks, mash everything together with a fork or use a hand mixer until smooth (1 tsp mustard, 3 tbsp mayo, 1 tsp vinegar or lemon juice, Pinch of salt and black pepper, Optional: dash of hot sauce or seasoning to taste) )
  10. Spoon the filling back into the egg white halves, OR pipe it in with a zip-top bag (snip the corner) for that fancy look.
  11. Sprinkle with paprika (classic), or try chives, crispy bacon bits, or a light drizzle of sriracha.
  12. Chill for 15‚Äì30 minutes before serving, or serve right away if you're feeling impatient.
